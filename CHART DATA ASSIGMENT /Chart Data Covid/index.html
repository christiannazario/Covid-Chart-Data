The Bronx has the least amount of people boosted with COVID-19 as Blacks rank lowest demographically.

The Bronx, NY-According to nyc.gov, the Bronx is the least vaccinated Borough with boosters, and many residents don’t seem interested in getting the booster either, as COVID-19 testing tents are placed across the Borough. A few people, including those who work in a covid testing tent, give different reasons why blacks are the lowest demographic to get the covid booster.

Licensed practical nurse Melissa Lyttle who works in mobile testing outside of Yankee stadium, thinks it’s the lack of trust in the government. “I don't really know, but it's probably the lack of trust with the government.” Said Melissa Lyttle

There are over a million people in the Bronx and only one zip code in Bronx County Riverdale has over 50% of people boosted. Riverdale is also one of the least populated areas in The Bronx. Additionally, the Bronx, which has almost a million more people than Staten Island, has 4% fewer people vaccinated than Staten Island.
